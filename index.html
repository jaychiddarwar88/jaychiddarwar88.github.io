Digital Time Capsule

"TimeCapsule" is a nostalgic digital app designed to safeguard memories for future revelation. Users can encapsulate photos, texts, and media, setting a date for when these mementos resurface as cherished recollections. It's a personal vault where memories are time-locked until the chosen moment of unwrapping. The app promises an added layer of anticipation as capsules, once opened, cannot be revisited or screenshotted, ensuring a singular, authentic experience. While currently local to your device, future iterations aim to include cloud storage and sharing capabilities, allowing others to partake in your curated time-specific surprises, only accessible when the countdown concludes.
